Share Your Y Story With The World

Whether the story is about you, a family member, someone you know or someone you know of…whether it’s about an individual, a group or an entire community…whether it happened recently or some time ago…as long as a YMCA had a small or large part in what happened, then please share it.

  • Whether the story is motivating, amusing or anything else - if it has meaning for you, then please share it.
  • Whether the story is a simple one or something more, if you want to tell it we are sure it’s worth hearing.
  • Whatever the story and whatever role the YMCA plays in it, if you appreciate it, then others will too.

Millions of people, children and adults, have had a notable Y related experience, so don’t worry about how good a writer you are. Just tell us in your own words.

The only thing that’s important is that other people get the chance to enjoy or benefit from what you have to say.

All stories will be posted online at YMCA.net.

People of all ages are encouraged to contribute. Parent or guardian must submit stories on behalf of minors.
